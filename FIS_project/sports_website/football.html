football is England's most popular sport, celebrated globally for its quality and iconic clubs. The 1966 World Cup win on home soil highlights its cultural significance
· Scoring:
     In football, a goal is worth 1 point. The team with the most goals wins the match.
· Pitch:
     Football is played on a rectangular grass or artificial turf field, with two goalposts at each end.
· Objective:
     Score more goals than the opposing team within 90 minutes of play (two 45-minute halves).
· Major Competitions:
     FIFA World Cup, UEFA Champions League, Copa America, and Premier League.

History Of football
     The origins of football can be traced back to ancient civilizations, where games involving a ball were played using feet. Modern football, or soccer, emerged in England during the mid-19th century when schools and clubs began standardizing rules. In 1863, the Football Association (FA) was formed in England
