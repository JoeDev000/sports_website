Basketball is a fast-paced, high-intensity sport played by two teams of five players each.
· Scoring:
     A field goal is worth 2 points if taken inside the three-point line, and 3 points if taken from beyond the three-point line..
· Pitch:
     The court is typically 94 feet long and 50 feet wide (for NBA games). The court has a three-point line and a free-throw line
· Objective:
     The primary goal is to score points by putting the ball through the opponent’s hoop. A field goal is worth either 2 or 3 points, depending on where the shot is taken, and a free throw is worth 1 point.
· Major Competitions:
     NBA, FIBA, African Basketball Club Competitions.

History Of basketball
     The history of basketball began with its invention in 1891 in Springfield, Massachusetts, by Canadian physical education instructor James Naismith as a less injury-prone sport than football. The game spread rapidly, and its popularity grew by the beginning of the 20th century, first in America and then around the world. The National Basketball Association (NBA), founded in 1946, had grown into a multibillion-dollar enterprise by the end of the century, and basketball had become an integral part of American culture.

2024 Basketball Events

NBA

The premier professional basketball league in the United States, featuring teams like the Los Angeles Lakers, Boston Celtics, Golden State Warriors, and Miami Heat.​

FIBA

The most prestigious international basketball event, outside of the Olympics. Teams from around the world compete for the title of world champions.​

EuroLeague

The premier professional basketball league in Europe. EuroLeague clubs compete in a regular season and playoff format, culminating in the EuroLeague Final Four, which determines the best team in Europe.​

BAL

The BAL was officially launched in 2021 as a collaboration between FIBA and the NBA. It is the first-ever NBA-sanctioned professional basketball league in Africa.​

African Basketball Club Competitions

These competitions help develop basketball at the club level in Africa and provide teams with opportunities to compete on both regional and international stages.​

The Egyptian Basketball Cup

It is a knockout-style tournament featuring basketball clubs from the Egyptian Basketball Super League (BSL), which is the top-tier professional basketball league in the country.
